## 4. Description — English (4000 chars)

Affinity is the social network built for adventurous couples who want
to meet other couples they actually trust.

Every profile is verified. Every interaction is private. The community
is 21+ only and moderated by real people.

**What makes Affinity different**

• Verified profiles — every couple completes a short video verification
  reviewed by our team before joining the main feed. No fake profiles,
  no solo users pretending to be a couple.

• Couple-first design — you register as a couple and act as a couple.
  One shared account, shared conversations, shared trips.

• Travel Match — planning a trip to a resort or cruise? Tell Affinity
  your dates and destination, and we'll show you other couples travelling
  there at the same time. Connections feel natural, not forced.

• Smart filters — age, distance, shared interests, shared dynamics.
  Find couples whose vibe actually matches yours, not a random list.

• Privacy first — screenshot protection on sensitive screens, no local
  image cache, invisible watermarking, bidirectional silent blocking.
  Your conversations stay yours.

• Real moderation — a human reviews every report. Users who cross the
  line are suspended, not warned.

**Built for**

Couples 21+ who want to meet other couples — not strangers, not
singles. Whether you're travelling, exploring new cities, or just
looking for like-minded friends, Affinity is the quieter, safer way
to connect.

**Verified, moderated, 21+**

Affinity enforces its age rating in-app during registration. Content
is curated, reports are acted on within hours, and our moderation
policy is published publicly.

**Available in Spanish and English.**

Got feedback? We read every message. Tap Profile → Help to reach us.
