Privacy Policy

Effective date: to be confirmed at launch

Disclaimer: this document is a developer-authored template and must be reviewed by a licensed attorney before the app ships. It is not legal advice.

1. Who we are

Affinity is a social platform exclusively for couples who have reached the age of 21. The service is operated by the Affinity team (affinitysocialclub@gmail.com).

2. What we collect

  • Account info: email address, password hash, OAuth identifiers (Google / Apple).
  • Couple profile: names, dates of birth, heights, city/country, description, photos, interest tags, and an identity-verification video.
  • Location: the city/country you enter (geocoded to a coarse latitude/longitude/geohash for proximity matching). We do not use continuous background GPS.
  • User content: messages you send, message requests, trip entries.
  • Moderation data: reports filed by or against you, blocks you create.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports and anonymised performance metrics via Firebase.

3. Verification video retention

When you submit an identity-verification video, a moderator reviews it manually. Approved videos are automatically deleted 7 days after approval. After deletion we keep only a cryptographic hash (SHA-256) of the video, the verification date, the moderator ID, and 2–3 very low-resolution static frames as an audit record.

4. Screenshot protection

Inside the app we enable platform-level screenshot and recording protection (Android FLAG_SECURE; iOS secure-canvas overlay) on sensitive screens. We also embed an invisible watermark in rendered photos so a leaked image can be traced back to the viewer couple.

5. Reporter confidentiality

When you report another couple, your identity is never disclosed to the reported couple, directly or indirectly. Only internal moderators can see who filed a report.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account from Profile → Account settings → Delete account. Deletion uses a 30-day grace period: your profile is hidden immediately, and you can cancel the deletion by signing in again within the window. On day 30, a Cloud Function permanently erases your couples document, photos, verification video, conversations, message requests, blocks, and Firebase Auth user.

7. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with infrastructure providers (Google Firebase, Google Cloud Platform) strictly to operate the service, and with law-enforcement authorities when legally compelled.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing affinitysocialclub@gmail.com. Most deletion and data-export operations are self-serve inside the app.

9. Contact

For any privacy question: affinitysocialclub@gmail.com
