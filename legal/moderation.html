Moderation Policy

How we keep the community safe

Affinity uses a hybrid moderation model: proactive identity verification on entry, active user reporting once inside, and human-review of every serious flag. This page exists so prospective app-store reviewers, law enforcement, and users themselves can see exactly how the system works.

1. Verification at entry

Every new couple must record a short video showing both partners together, stating the current date. A human moderator reviews every video and approves or rejects with a stated reason. Rejection after two attempts permanently blocks the pair from signing up again.

2. Reporting

Any user may report another couple via the ⋮ menu inside a chat. Categories:

  • Fake profile — impersonation or misleading setup
  • Harassment — abusive, threatening, or intimidating messages
  • Non-consensual content — private images or content shared without permission
  • Suspected minor — any suspicion a user is under 21 (zero tolerance — highest priority)
  • Spam — commercial or repetitive unwanted content
  • Other — with mandatory free-text description

3. Automatic safeguards

  • Reporting a couple immediately blocks them from your experience.
  • 5 reports from different sources within 30 days auto-escalate the reported couple to moderator review.
  • Filing 10+ reports within 7 days temporarily disables your reporting capability to prevent misuse.
  • Reporter identity is never disclosed to the reported couple.

4. Blocking

Blocks are bidirectional and silent: both couples disappear from each other's experience, and the blocked party is never notified.

5. Response times

Target response times:

  • Suspected-minor reports: within 4 hours
  • Harassment / non-consensual content: within 24 hours
  • Other categories: within 72 hours

Actual performance depends on moderation team capacity during MVP and will be published as we collect operational data.

6. Actions available to moderators

  • Dismiss — no action, report closed
  • Warning — in-app notice to the reported couple with category only, never reporter identity
  • Temporary suspension — hidden from discovery pending investigation
  • Permanent ban — account deactivated; applied only on clear policy violations

7. Appeals

If your couple has been suspended or banned and you believe the decision was incorrect, email affinitysocialclub@gmail.com with your account email and a brief explanation. We respond within 5 business days.

8. Contact

For moderation inquiries: affinitysocialclub@gmail.com
